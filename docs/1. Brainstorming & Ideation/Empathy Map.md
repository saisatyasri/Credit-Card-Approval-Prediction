## Key Insight

Riya represents millions of first-time credit card applicants who are **uninformed** about eligibility criteria and **anxious** about rejection. The Credit Card Approval Prediction system directly addresses her needs by providing instant, transparent eligibility feedback — empowering her to make informed decisions before submitting a formal application.
